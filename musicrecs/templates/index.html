Welcome to Musicrecs!

Musicrecs is a platform for sharing spotify music recommendations anonymously with friends.

To get started, create a new music recommendation round and then share the created round link with others.

The recommendation round will have three phases:

Submit Phase: Participants submit spotify music links of their recommendations.

Listen Phase: Participants listen to the music and consider who may have submitted what.

Revealed Phase: It is revealed which participants recommended what.

"Snoozin" will automatically send in a recommendation for every round with something he's been listening to lately.

FAQs

Who is "snoozin"?

"Snoozin" is the sobriquet for the collection of Spotify API powered algorithms used to add an additional music recommendation to each round.

Do I need a Spotify account to participate?

No, although it definitely enhances the experience to have an account. Spotify allows people without accounts to search and share music, which is all that is necessary to participate.
